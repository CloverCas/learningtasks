ABOUT US

  "Your Mental Health Matters!" webpage is authored by Casandra Heaven Estelloso of BSIT1-B in line with Midterm Culminating Learning Evidence. This movement was created especially to assist persons who are suffering from mental health issues. It defines what it means to be afflicted with mental disease. This movement demonstrates to persons suffering from mental illnesses that they are not alone and that there is always hope. It is critical that you work to reduce stigma, fight for mental health reform, and help individuals afflicted by mental illness live better lives. Let us stand shoulder to shoulder to encourage one another.
